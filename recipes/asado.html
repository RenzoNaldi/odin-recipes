Asado

Description

The Argentinian Asado is more than just grilling meat—it’s a tradition, a gathering, and a way of life. Cooked slowly over embers, the meat becomes tender, flavorful, and deliciously smoky. Perfect for sharing with friends and family!

Ingredients

  • Beef cuts
  • Coarse salt
  • Charcoal or wood

Steps

  1. Prepare the grill
  2. Season the meat
  3. Cook slowly
  4. flip once
  5. Check doneness
