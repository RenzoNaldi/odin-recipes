Choripan

Description

The Choripán is a classic Argentinian street food—simple, flavorful, and perfect for any barbecue. Juicy grilled chorizo inside a crispy bread roll, topped with chimichurri or salsa criolla. A must-try!

Ingredients

  • 2 pork chorizos
  • 2 bread rolls
  • chimichurri or salsa criolla

Steps

  1. Grill the chorizo
  2. Cut the chorizo
  3. Toast the bread
  4. Assemble
